## Sequence 9: Sailor (Keeper of the Seas)\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Flordofthemysteries.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FTyrant_Pathway%2FAbilities%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

**Traits**<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_tyrant_abilities_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_tyrant_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Aquatic Affinity**: **Sailors** possess certain attributes commonly found in sea creatures.
    -   Even without any equipment, they are able to remain underwater for at least ten minutes.
    -   **Sailors** can swim through the sea like fish and move underwater with the agility of marine animals.
    -   They are able to dive 15 meters into the **Water** without any protection or equipment.

-   **Illusory Scales**: They possess illusory scales hidden beneath their skin.
    -   These scales makes them slippery and as hard to grasp as fish.<sup id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2" aria-label="Reference 2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   They also help a **Sailor** resist a certain degree of damage.<sup id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3" aria-label="Reference 3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Balance**: A **Sailor** possesses an exceptional sense of **Balance**.
    -   Even on a boat during a rainstorm, a **Sailor** can walk around freely as though standing on solid, stable ground.<sup id="cite_ref-1.6_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.6-4" aria-label="Reference 4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Physical Enhancement**: **Sailors** possess outstanding strength.

    -   They are now able to freely extract oxygen from the water.
    -   Underwater, they fear no [Beyonder](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Beyonders "Beyonders") below [Demigod](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Sequence "Sequence") level.<sup id="cite_ref-2.785_22-1"><a href="#cite_note-2.785-22" aria-label="Reference 22"><span>[</span>22<span>]</span></a></sup>

        -   It moves at an unavoidable speed, as if it were from a god of thunder.
        -   The steps involved in a **Lightning Bolt Arrow** are as follows.
            -   Their hair unfurls, defying nature, each strand distinct and swirling with **Lightning**. Upon drawing a bow, the sky darkens, clouds gather, and **Lightning** dances.
            -   They then release the silver-white **Lightning** from their hair, entwining it with the arrow before releasing the string and shooting the arrow out.
            -   Dark clouds lump the sky and a thick **Lightning Bolt** descends superimposing itself on the arrow, making it completely silver white.
    -   **Lightning Strike**: An **Ocean Songster** can produce silver-white electric light capable of paralysis, generating a **Lightning Strike** to smite their targets.<sup id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26" aria-label="Reference 26"><span>[</span>26<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-27"><a href="#cite_note-27" aria-label="Reference 27"><span>[</span>27<span>]</span></a></sup>

        -   It can't be dodged—only preemptively avoided. Even if it does not hit, it will spread to nearby conductive materials in the form of countless small "electric snakes".
            -   **Lightning** makes even non-humanoid individuals (such as **Shadow** creatures) be paralyzed.<sup id="cite_ref-2.784_28-1"><a href="#cite_note-2.784-28" aria-label="Reference 28"><span>[</span>28<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Singing**: The ability to influence targets with one's voice, it varies based on the user's individual traits.
    -   This ability will be different because of every **Ocean Songster's** uniqueness, producing different branching paths, which includes:
        1.  Using beautiful **Singing** to interfere with the enemy's [Spirit Body](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Soul "Soul"), causing them to turn adrift and fall into a daze, or to enhance one's own explosive power.

            -   With [Alger Wilson's](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Alger_Wilson "Alger Wilson") **Singing**, [Gehrman](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Klein_Moretti "Klein Moretti") who already stuffed his ears and converged his **Spirituality** suddenly felt his forehead’s blood vessels throb, instantly having the urge to kill the singer and destroy everything before him.<sup id="cite_ref-1.808_19-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.808-19" aria-label="Reference 19"><span>[</span>19<span>]</span></a></sup>

This is because this includes an "exchange" at the spiritual level, making it difficult to defend against.<sup id="cite_ref-31"><a href="#cite_note-31" aria-label="Reference 31"><span>[</span>31<span>]</span></a></sup>
