## Sequence 9: Bard\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Flordofthemysteries.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSun_Pathway%2FAbilities%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D1&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

**Traits**

-   **Physical Enhancement**: Upon advancement, their physical condition would be strengthened.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

**New Abilities**

-   **Singing**:<sup>⁂</sup> They can imbue all sorts of effects to themselves and allies through their **Singing**, a sort of "job" that brought about devotion and submission.<sup id="cite_ref-1.140_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.140-2" aria-label="Reference 2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   **Bards** can imbue courage, dispel **Fear**, enhance strength, as well as bring forth a degree of spiritual recovery, agility, speed, and other attributes through their **Singing**.
        -   For believers of the [Eternal Blazing Sun](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Eternal_Blazing_Sun "Eternal Blazing Sun"), a **Bard's Singing** will also have the effect of deepening their piety and improving their obedience.
    -   Apart from being able to bestow physical enhancements, courage, bring forth a degree of spiritual recovery, etc. their **Singing** isn't able to do anything else.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

## Sequence 8: Light Suppliant\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Flordofthemysteries.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSun_Pathway%2FAbilities%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D2&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

**New Abilities**

-   They can use a limited number of **Spells** from the **Sun Domain**, consisting of **Light** and **Fire**, that are effective against the undead and other [Evil Spirits](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Evil_Spirits "Evil Spirits").<sup id="cite_ref-1.140_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.140-2" aria-label="Reference 2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   **Sunshine**: A **Light Suppliant** can create a **Light** like the scorching **Sun** at noon, which can cause great damage to corpses and ghosts, and affect the vision of other creatures.
    -   **Blessing**: They can **Bless** themselves and their allies, reducing the effects of **Fear**, **Cold**, **Darkness**, and **Death** effects while enhancing their damage towards the undead.
    -   **Daytime**: A **Light Suppliant** can allow the surrounding _ten meters_ to receive **Light**, and this produced **Light** will naturally spread to further distances.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3" aria-label="Reference 3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4" aria-label="Reference 4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   **Night Vision**: A **Light Suppliant** can light two "miniature **Suns**" in their eyes to see through **Darkness** and thus achieve **Night Vision**.<sup id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5" aria-label="Reference 5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Knowledge (Mysticism)**: They have knowledge of [Ritualistic Magic](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Ritualistic_Magic "Ritualistic Magic") within the **Sun Domain**.
    -   Some things that can only be accomplished in later **Sequences** can be done by a **Light Supplicant** through their [Ritualistic Magic](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Ritualistic_Magic "Ritualistic Magic") knowledge.
        -   They can summon a pure beam of **Fiery** **Light** through this.
        -   They are able to make **Holy Water** using [Rituals](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Ritualistic_Magic "Ritualistic Magic").

-   **Evil Detection**:<sup>⁂</sup> They can sense the presence of undead creatures and evil entities.<sup id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6" aria-label="Reference 6"><span>[</span>6<span>]</span></a></sup>

## Sequence 7: Solar High Priest\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Flordofthemysteries.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSun_Pathway%2FAbilities%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D3&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

**Traits**

-   **Physical Enhancement**: A **Solar High Priest's** body becomes stronger as well as gains considerable resistance against ailments and treacherous environments.<sup id="cite_ref-1.584_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.584-7" aria-label="Reference 7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup>

**New Abilities**

-   They gain numerous theurgical abilities within the **Sun** **Domain** that can truly **Purify** depraved influences. The divine knowledge that fills their mind includes these following new **Spells**.<sup id="cite_ref-1.584_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.584-7" aria-label="Reference 7"><span>[</span>7<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   **Sun Halo**: They can form a _golden halo_ around their head that enhances the courage of their companions within a _20 meter distance_ as well as **Purifies** the **Evil** energies in them.
    -   **Sun Holy Water**: **Solar High Priests** can imbue ordinary water with this spell to turn it into **Sun Holy Water** that can **Exorcise** [Evil Spirits](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Evil_Spirits "Evil Spirits"), drive away the cold, and **Purify** wraiths.
    -   **Holy Oath**: Upon silently reciting the relevant ancient words, a **Solar High Priest** can temporarily strengthen one’s own strength, agility, fire damage, and holy damage.
    -   **Holy Light Summoning**: **Solar High Priests** can summon a holy beam of **Light** from the sky to **Purify** the undead and inflict certain damage on other normal targets.
    -   **Cleave of Purification**: They are able to imbue their physical attacks, weapons, and bullets with this **Purification** effect that is especially effective on wraiths.
    -   **Horror Immunity**: Upon activation by a **Solar High Priest**, a warm power will instantly fill the body, making the target no longer feel any **Fear**.
        -   **Horror Immunity** works both on passive influences and also on active mental attacks done by **Evil** influences that aim to hurt one's psyche and/or cause one to falter.
    -   **Fire of Light**: **Solar High Priests** are able to instantly produce dense, golden, and holy **Flames** instantaneously at a target location.
        -   These golden illusory **Flames** exude the majestic and pervasive aura of the **Sun**, and because of their density, they will form an ocean.

-   Despite their expertise in **Purification** and **Exorcism**, those effects are not omnipotent.<sup id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8" aria-label="Reference 8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   The power of **Purification** and **Exorcism** works in the category of **Evil**, which includes the aura of depravity but not the undead. The latter isn't necessarily **Evil**, but can be **Purified** and **Exorcised** because it is incompatible with the real world and needs to return to the [Spirit World](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Spirit_World "Spirit World").
        -   Therefore, **Purifying** and **Exorcising** the undead will have less of an effect than doing it on distinctly **Evil** influences; however, in most cases it hardly makes a difference.
    -   Birthing children is not an **Evil** act for the [Mother Goddess of Depravity](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Mother_Goddess_of_Depravity) but a **Sacred** one, making it immune to **Purification**; however, transforming those infants into the **Undead** will make them susceptible to **Purification's** power. This wont change even after becoming an [Angel](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Angels "Angels").<sup id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9" aria-label="Reference 9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup>

**Strengthened Abilities**<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   The **Spell** and sacrifice abilities within this domain have been greatly enhanced.

## Sequence 6: Notary\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Flordofthemysteries.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSun_Pathway%2FAbilities%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D4&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

**Description**

The effectiveness of their **Contracts** promoted the development of business, and thus **Notaries** became an important part of the entire business, As such there were rewards of wealth and status. This is an indirect benefit, which was enviously called "**Fortune**" by the [Church of the Fool](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Church_of_the_Fool "Church of the Fool").

**Traits**

-   **Physical Enhancement**: **Notaries** receive a massive boost in their already strong physical attributes.
    -   Despite this, they still are more focused on playing a support role.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

**New Abilities**

-   **Notarization**: By utilizing a language that stirs the powers of nature along with the relevant words, a **Notary** can either achieve **Authentication**, **Amplification**, and **Nullification**.<sup id="cite_ref-1.767_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.767-10" aria-label="Reference 10"><span>[</span>10<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   **Authentication**: Through their **Notarization**, a **Notary** is able to determine the authenticity of various things such as potion formulas, as well as enforce **Contracts**.
        -   Once a signature is provided as confirmation, even a **Sequence 5 Beyonder** cannot violate it, and a **Sequence 4 Demigod** would have great difficulty breaking the **Contract**.<sup id="cite_ref-1.767_10-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.767-10" aria-label="Reference 10"><span>[</span>10<span>]</span></a></sup>
            -   If a **Sequence 4 Demigod** tries to forcefully violate it, they will have to pay quite a price.
            -   Those who lie or break the **Contract** would be engulfed in burning golden flames.<sup id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11" aria-label="Reference 11"><span>[</span>11<span>]</span></a></sup>
        -   Unlike an **[Imperative Mage](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Justiciar_Pathway/Abilities#Sequence_4:_Imperative_Mage)**, the binding force of a **Notary's** **Contracts** comes from the witness of [Gods](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Gods "Gods") or higher beings, rather than the rule-based **Order**.<sup id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12" aria-label="Reference 12"><span>[</span>12<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   **Amplification**: A valid **Notarization** of an ability, temporarily enhancing a targeted **Beyonder** power. Used by proclaiming "_God says it's effective!_".
        -   **Notaries** can do this to their own **Beyonder** powers as well, greatly enhancing the potency of their **Purification** and **Exorcism** capabilities.
    -   **Nullification**: An invalid **Notarization** of an ability, weakening or forcefully dispersing a targeted **Beyonder** power. Used by proclaiming _"God says it's ineffective!"_.
    -   Powers of all kind, including verbal command abilities, authorities (**Mutation**, **Wishes**, etc.), and traits (**Sovereign of Spirits**) can be affected by **Notarization**.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-6"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

## Sequence 5: Priest of Light\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Flordofthemysteries.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSun_Pathway%2FAbilities%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D5&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

**Description**

A **Priest of Light** masters various divine **Spells** in the **Sun** **Domain** and can **Purify** the undead and filthy creatures within a certain range. They are the nemesis of filth and the undead.

**Strengthened Abilities**<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-7"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   Among the divine **Spells** they have mastered, two divine **Spells** that were previously mastered have undergone qualitative changes, and the other **Spells** have also been enhanced in power.
    -   **Light of Holiness**: Calls forth a pure and blazing hot pillar of **Light**, which is extra effective against the undead. This is a stronger version of **Holy Light Summoning**.
        -   This pure, blazing **Light** column will descend from the sky, enveloping the **Priest of Light's** enemy and vaporizing their body.
            -   The additional effect is two miniature suns will appear in their eyes- the effect of **Night Vision** automatically applied.
        -   To activate this **Spell**, the **Priest of Light's** body will lean back with their arms open, as if they were embracing the **Sun**.
    -   **Purification Halo**: Produces a halo-like effect, **Purifying** all undead and foul creatures within a certain range. This is the stronger version of **Sun Halo**.
        -   It dyes the surrounding area golden, warms the companions around them, brings strong courage to allies, drives away **Evil**, as well as can be precisely controlled.

## Sequence 4: Unshadowed\[[](https://auth.fandom.com/signin?redirect=https%3A%2F%2Flordofthemysteries.fandom.com%2Fwiki%2FSun_Pathway%2FAbilities%3Fveaction%3Dedit%26section%3D6&uselang=en&metadata=article-registration-edit-article-section "Sign in to edit")\]

**Traits**<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-8"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Physical Enhancement**: They have evolved from an auxiliary clergyman into a more comprehensive **Sun** warrior, with qualitative changes in physique, strength, speed, agility, etc.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-9"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>
-   **Intuition**: As **Demigods** from the **Sun Domain**, they possess a sharp **Intuition** that helps them know if something has gone wrong.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1326_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.1326-13" aria-label="Reference 13"><span>[</span>13<span>]</span></a></sup>

**New Abilities**<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-10"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Purification**: This is the core power of an **Unshadowed**, allowing them to directly **Purify** all sorts of things without needing the relevant theurgical abilities.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1032_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.1032-14" aria-label="Reference 14"><span>[</span>14<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15" aria-label="Reference 15"><span>[</span>15<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-1.1255_16-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.1255-16" aria-label="Reference 16"><span>[</span>16<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   An **Unshadowed** can directly **Purify** any kind of **Degeneration**, **Corruption**, **Corrosion**, **Darkness**, **Evil**, **Aliments**, and other **Domains** that influences a person or area.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1032_14-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.1032-14" aria-label="Reference 14"><span>[</span>14<span>]</span></a></sup>
        -   This **Beyonder** power is extremely effective against wraiths, the fallen, and the unclean.
        -   **Purification** makes it very hard for a **[Parasite](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Error_Pathway/Abilities#Sequence_4:_Parasite)** to **Parasitize** an **Unshadowed**.<sup id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17" aria-label="Reference 17"><span>[</span>17<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18" aria-label="Reference 18"><span>[</span>18<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19" aria-label="Reference 19"><span>[</span>19<span>]</span></a></sup>
        -   **Purification** can melt the **Silver Rapier** of a **[Silver Knight](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Twilight_Giant_Pathway/Abilities#Sequence_3:_Silver_Knight)** [Evil Spirit](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Evil_Spirits "Evil Spirits").<sup id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20" aria-label="Reference 20"><span>[</span>20<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   Since **Beyonder Characteristics** are not a distinctly original part of a **Beyonder**, an **Unshadowed** can directly **Purify Beyonder Characteristics** from them.
        -   They can completely separate the highest sequence **Beyonder** **Characteristics** from a whole **Beyonder Characteristic**, making it _two_ _separate_ **Beyonder** **Characteristics**.
        -   An **Unshadowed** can directly rid of the mental pollution or unclean influences contained within a [Sealed Artifact](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Sealed_Artifact "Sealed Artifact") or **Beyonder Characteristic** through **Purification**.
        -   They can directly **Purify** a **Beyonder**, taking _1 hour_ to lower their **Sequence** by _1_. With _6 hours_, they can turn even a **Saint** to a normal person.
            -   An **Unshadowed** is able to recover **Beyonder Characteristics** from **Beyonders** who have failed to promote but are not yet dead or have lost control, helping them leave the mystical world and become an ordinary person again.
        -   They can **Purify** mental madness, suppressing the [Original Creator's](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Original_Creator "Original Creator") spiritual imprint, however, there are hidden dangers, for example, making others more extreme.<sup id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21" aria-label="Reference 21"><span>[</span>21<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Unshadowed Domain**: They can let a bright blazing **Light** illuminate every corner in a region, removing all darkness and revealing all **Concealed** objects within it. It also has **Purification** effects, weakening the **Beyonder** powers of **Corruption**, **Degeneration**, and **Darkness**.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1254_22-0"><a href="#cite_note-1.1254-22" aria-label="Reference 22"><span>[</span>22<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   It is effective at revealing the invisibility created from a **[Silver Knight's](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Twilight_Giant_Pathway/Abilities#Sequence_3:_Silver_Knight)** **Light Concealment**.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1254_22-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.1254-22" aria-label="Reference 22"><span>[</span>22<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   The **Unshadowed Domain** can also defend against **Darkness** and **Evil Domain** attacks.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-11"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   An **Unshadowed Domain** does not possess a barrier in the physical sense.<sup id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23" aria-label="Reference 23"><span>[</span>23<span>]</span></a></sup>
        -   Anyone could leave the domain or exert influence on the outside world.

-   **Flaring Sun**: An **Unshadowed** can call forth a huge ball of pure **Light** condensed by countless sacred flames instantaneously at a target location, shrouding and setting their enemies ablaze.
    -   It inflicts great damage to those with **Corruption**, **Degeneration**, and **Undying** attributes.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1255_16-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.1255-16" aria-label="Reference 16"><span>[</span>16<span>]</span></a></sup>
        -   The **Flaring Sun** can melt the **Silver Armor** of a **[Silver Knight](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Twilight_Giant_Pathway/Abilities#Sequence_3:_Silver_Knight)**, though it wouldn't do decisive damage.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1255_16-2"><a href="#cite_note-1.1255-16" aria-label="Reference 16"><span>[</span>16<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   However, the attack range is large, and it is easy for them to accidentally hurt their own side.
    -   The gesture of activation is to open one's arms, as if embracing the gift of the gods.

-   **Holy Eye**:<sup>⁂</sup> They can infuse power into their eyes, allowing them to directly seek out _shadows_ of _cold_, _crimson_, or _fallen_ **Evil** in their surroundings through any physical obstructions.<sup id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24" aria-label="Reference 24"><span>[</span>24<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-1.1326_13-1"><a href="#cite_note-1.1326-13" aria-label="Reference 13"><span>[</span>13<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   The **Light** of their **Unshadowed Eye** allows them to shoot out a burning **Light** that **Purifies**.
    -   It can also allow them to directly see scenes of the surrounding areas through sunlight.

-   **Holy Equipment**:<sup>⁂</sup> An **Unshadowed** can cause a golden glow to surge out of their bodies, condensing a layer of armor made of pure **Light**, forming a full set of **Holy Armor**.
    -   A corresponding **Holy Weapon** of choice can be manifested, such as a huge mace of **Light**.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1255_16-3"><a href="#cite_note-1.1255-16" aria-label="Reference 16"><span>[</span>16<span>]</span></a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25" aria-label="Reference 25"><span>[</span>25<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Unshadowed Spear**: An **Unshadowed** is able to condense an **Unshadowed Spear** made out of pure **Sunlight** to surgically strike their foes from afar.
    -   Upon contact or after being thrown a certain distance, an **Unshadowed Spear** would explode to a mass of brilliant **Light** like a miniature **Sun**.<sup id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26" aria-label="Reference 26"><span>[</span>26<span>]</span></a></sup>
        -   This is especially devastating against those who are weak to **Purification** damage.
        -   The miniature **Sun** is pure white and bright enough to illuminate a city.<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-12"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Solar Oath**: They can apply various enhancements to oneself and allies within a certain range.

-   **Pure White Ray**: They can condense **Light** into a scorching and dazzling ray attack.

**Strengthened Abilities**<sup id="cite_ref-wechat_sun_abilities_1-13"><a href="#cite_note-wechat_sun_abilities-1" aria-label="Reference 1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup>

-   **Notarization**: Their **Notarization** powers are massively enhanced, its compatibility with their other **Beyonder** powers allow them to **Amplify** their already strong **Purification** capabilities.<sup id="cite_ref-1.1255_16-4"><a href="#cite_note-1.1255-16" aria-label="Reference 16"><span>[</span>16<span>]</span></a></sup>
    -   **Ampliifcation**: The **God says** ability can be utilized more precisely, and through the use of specific lines, it is able to avoid potential ambiguity regarding various abilities.<sup id="cite_ref-27"><a href="#cite_note-27" aria-label="Reference 27"><span>[</span>27<span>]</span></a></sup>
        -   [Lato Guiaro](chrome-extension://kcbaglhfgbkjdnpeokaamjjkddempipm/wiki/Ultraman "Ultraman") chose "_God says entering dreams is ineffective!_" over "_sleeping is ineffective_" because the former is more specific and avoids ambiguity.
